Congratulations, you have been awarded the Chancellor’s Letter of Commendation
Dear Dhruvil Bhavinbhai

I am writing to commend you on the outstanding academic results you achieved toward the Chancellor's Letter of Commendation.

As a result of your achievement, you have been awarded the Chancellor’s Letter of Commendation for 2024. This award recognises students with a cumulative program Grade Point Average in the top 5 per cent of undergraduate or postgraduate students in their Academic Unit.

This achievement will be noted on your academic transcript.

The Chancellor’s Letter of Commendation is a prestigious acknowledgement of your academic excellence and outstanding contribution to the UniSA community.

As a recipient of the Chancellor’s Letter, you are eligible to join the Golden Key International Honour Society.

Golden Key offers access to scholarships and grants, career advice and experience, and the opportunity to become part of an international network of high achieving graduate and undergraduate students.

An invitation to join will be sent to you by the Society in the next a few months. Information about the Society is available on the Golden Key website and if you have any questions about your invitation please contact golden.key@unisa.edu.au.

My sincere congratulations on your academic performance and the dedicated effort required to achieve it. I wish you every success in your academic future.

If you have any enquiries regarding this award please don’t hesitate to email us scholarships.prizes@unisa.edu.au.

Yours sincerely


The Hon John Hill
Chancellor
